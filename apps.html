About

Our Storybook helps kids turn their ideas into personalized storybooks.

Start with a drawing, transform it into an AI-generated image, and add your own words to build a story page by page.

With Our Storybook, kids can:

  • Draw their own characters, animals, and scenes
  • Generate colorful images inspired by their drawings
  • Write or tell stories in their own words
  • Create personalized books from their imagination
  • Enjoy creative play through art and storytelling

Made for young creators

Our Storybook is designed to be simple, playful, and creative. Kids can make dinosaur adventures, bedtime stories, funny animal tales, and more — all from their own ideas.


Whether used independently or together with a parent, Our Storybook makes it easy to create a book that feels personal and fun.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Overview

Our Storybook ("the App") is designed for children and their families. We take privacy seriously and have built the App to minimize data collection. This policy describes what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

Information We Collect

The App does not require account creation or the collection of personal information to use its core features. The following data may be processed:

  • Drawings and images you create within the App, processed to generate AI images (not stored on our servers after generation)
  • Text entered by the user for story pages (stored locally on your device only)
  • Standard usage data collected by Apple through the App Store (subject to Apple's privacy policy)

Children's Privacy

Our Storybook is intended for use by children under the supervision of a parent or guardian. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. All content is stored locally on the device and is not transmitted to external servers except as described in this policy.

AI Image Generation

When a user requests an AI-generated image, the drawing or prompt is sent to a third-party AI service to produce the image. This data is used solely to generate the image and is not retained, sold, or used for any other purpose. Please review Google's privacy policy for more details on how Gemini handles data.

Data Storage

Storybooks, images, and written content are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage mechanisms. We do not operate servers that store your creative content. You may delete the App and all associated data at any time through your device settings.

Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Apple App Store (distribution and optional in-app purchases)
  • Google Gemini (AI image generation)

Your Rights

Since personal data is stored locally on your device, you retain full control. You can delete the App and all its data at any time. If you have questions or concerns about data handled by third-party services, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
